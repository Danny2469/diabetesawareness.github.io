Learn About Diabetes

Understanding diabetes is essential for managing your health effectively. Below, we provide key information about diabetes types, symptoms, management, and support.

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic health condition that occurs when the body cannot effectively use insulin, a hormone that regulates blood sugar levels. There are two main types of diabetes: Type 1 and Type 2.

Symptoms of Diabetes

Common symptoms of diabetes include excessive thirst, frequent urination, extreme fatigue, and blurred vision. If you experience any of these symptoms, consult a healthcare provider.

Management of Diabetes

Managing diabetes typically involves a combination of healthy eating, regular physical activity, blood sugar monitoring, and medication as prescribed by a healthcare professional.

Support and Resources

Many organizations offer support and resources for those living with diabetes. Reach out to local diabetes associations or online communities for assistance and guidance.
